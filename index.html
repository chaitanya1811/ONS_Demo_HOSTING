Rice (A Diverse Range of Rice Varieties)

Description: Rice is a staple in many cultures, and we offer a comprehensive range of rice varieties to meet your specific culinary preferences. Our rice offerings span both non-basmati and basmati ranges.

Non-Basmati Rice: We provide various non-basmati rice types, including IR64 Raw and Parboiled, Sona Masuri, Parmal PDS, and 100% Broken Rice Sortex and Non-Sortex. Our direct tie-ups with mills across different regions in India, including Raipur (Chhattisgarh), Bardhaman (West Bengal), Gondia (Maharashtra), Rewa (Madhya Pradesh), and Telangana, ensure a steady supply of high-quality rice for domestic purposes.

Basmati Rice: In the basmati category, we offer a wide selection, including 1121 Basmati Rice, 1718 Basmati Rice, 1509 Basmati Rice, Sugandha Basmati Rice, PR-14 Basmati Rice, and Pusa Basmati Rice. Our basmati rice is renowned for its aroma and long, slender grains, making it ideal for various culinary creations.

MSA Traders' Role: We are exporters of rice, catering to domestic and international markets. Our MOQ for rice exports is 25 metric tons. We take pride in our robust procurement capabilities and sourcing the finest quality rice for our clients.


Payment Terms

We understand the importance of flexible payment options, and at MSA Traders, we offer three distinct payment terms to accommodate various needs:

1. 100% Advance RTGS: Ideal for products like FMCG products and RBD Palm Olein Oil, we request full payment before delivery to ensure a seamless transaction.

2. 100% Irrevocable SBLC: If you prefer the security of an irrevocable Standby Letter of Credit, we are ready to work with this payment method.

3. 100% on Lowry Receipt: This payment term is tailored for the procurement of limited and niche agro commodities.

Please note: We do not provide goods on a credit basis.
